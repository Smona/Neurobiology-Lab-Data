What underlies the depolarizing ramp at the beginning of the action potential?
This depolarizing ramp is the charging of the membrane by the pulse of stimulus current. You can change the amplitude or duration of this pulse by selecting "IClamp" in the Stimulus Control panel. This selection will bring up the values of the current pulse in the lower half of the window.

The Membrane Tutorial gives a more detailed explanation of how the current pulse charges the membrane.
